Puff Pastry

Description:


Puff pastry is a light, flaky dough made by layering butter between thin sheets of dough, then folding and rolling it repeatedly to create multiple layers. When baked, it puffs up and becomes golden and crisp, making it perfect for both sweet and savory dishes like croissants, turnovers, and vol-au-vents. It‚Äôs a versatile, delicious pastry that adds a buttery, melt-in-your-mouth texture to any recipe.

How to use:


To use puff pastry, thaw it if frozen for 2 hours before use. Roll it out on a floured surface, then cut and shape it as needed. Add your desired filling (sweet or savory), seal the edges, brush with egg wash for a golden finish, and bake according to the recipe until it puffs up and turns golden brown.



Ingredients:


Enriched wheat flour, Water, Vegetable fats [Palm oil, Palm stearin, Soybean oil, Water, Salt, Emulsifiers: (Mono and di-glycerides of fatty acids, Polyglycerides of fatty acids), Acidity regulator: Citric acid, Coloring: Beta-carotene, Flavouring], Salt.
