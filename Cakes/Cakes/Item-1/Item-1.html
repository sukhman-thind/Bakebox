Red Mills Gluten Free
Egg Replacer

Description:


Red Mill's Gluten-Free Egg Replacer is an easy-to-use substitute for eggs in baking. It helps create light, moist texture in gluten-free, vegan, or egg-free recipes, making it perfect for cakes, cookies, muffins, and more. Simply mix with water to use!

More about this item


How to use:


For one egg mix 1 tbsp of the egg replacer with 2 tbsps of water or 15ml of egg replacer with 30ml of water. keep it a 1:2 ratio for for any amount needed.



Ingredients:


Potato Starch, Tapioca Flour, Baking Soda, Psyllium Husk Fiber.
