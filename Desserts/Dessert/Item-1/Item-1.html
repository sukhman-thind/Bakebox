RECIPE

APPLE TURNOVERS

Total Time: 35 minutes | Yield: 6 Turnovers

Ingredients
















Method

1.Wash, peel and cut the apple into thin slices.

2.Place sliced apples into cold water and add lemon juice.

3.In a sauce pan add a tbsp of butter, once melted, drain apples and add to the sauce pan. Let simmer for 2-5 minutes or until slightly soft.

4.Add brown sugar, cinnamon and simmer for 3 more minutes or until desired texture. Let apple mixture cool.

5.While apple mixture is cooling, lightly powder your counter and place puff pastry dough, and roll out slightly to get rid of creases.

6.Cut the pastry into 6 equal parts.

7.Once the apple mixture is cool, place a spoon full onto half of each piece cut prior.

8.Fold the half onto the side with the mixture and crimp with a fork, sealing it.

9.Do that with the rest of the mixture.

10.Optional , sprinkle white sugar on top for a shiny finish.

11.Place that into an air fryer and let air fry for roughly 5-7 minutes or until golden brown.

12.Let cool and enjoy!
