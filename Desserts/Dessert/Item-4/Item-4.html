RECIPE

BLUEBERRY MUFFINS

Total Time: 45 minutes | Yield: 8 Muffins


Ingredients




















Method

1.Preheat oven to 400F, and line a muffin tray with cupcake liners.

2.Make egg replacer ( mix 1 tbsp of egg replacer with 2 tbsp of water). Put aside for a minute.

3.Mix all the dry ingredients together ( flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt).

4.In another bowl mix wet ingredients including egg replacer, until well combined.

5.Add the wet ingredients to the dry and mix.

6.Add blue berries (If using frozen blue berries make sure to coat them in flour before adding to the mix).

7.Scoop about 1/3 cup into every other muffin space, leaving a space between each muffin. In the empty space add 1tbsp of water.

8.Place into oven and bake for roughly 20 minutes or until toothpick inserted comes out clean.

9.Let cool and enjoy!
