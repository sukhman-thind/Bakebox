RECIPE

CINNAMON ROLLS

Total Time: 35 minutes | Yield: 6 Turnovers

Ingredients

Dough















Filling









Method

Dough

1.In a bowl, microwave heat the milk and butter until butter is melted and liquid is warm.( warm enough to keep your finger in for at 10 seconds without it getting too hot to not kill the yeast).

2.After ensuring liquid is not too hot, add the yeast. Let yeast activate for 5-10 minutes, then add salt and sugar.

3.Add half the flour at a time and stir with a wooden spoon, once dough is too thick to stir transfer to a floured surface and knead until a loose, smooth ball forms.

4.Then place dough into an oiled bowl, over and place in a worm area to rest until dough has doubled in size.

5.After dough has risen, on a floured surface roll it out into a rectangle about 1 cm thick.

6.Brush the surface of the dough with melted butter and sprinkle with desired amount of the sugar and cinnamon mix.

7.Tightly roll up the dough, forming a lot, using a serrated knife cut the dough into 1- 1.5 inch portions and place into buttered pan.

8.Let the cinnamon rolls rest in the pan for an hour.

9.Preheat oven to 350F.

10.Place into oven for 20-25 minutes or until slightly golden brown.

11.Optional, add a drizzle of icing (mix of powdered sugar and milk).

12.Let cool slighlty and enjoy!
