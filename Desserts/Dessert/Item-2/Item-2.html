RECIPE

Puff Pastry Heart Cookies

Total Time: 20 minutes | Yield: 12-14 Cookies

Ingredients












Method

1.Roll out pastry dough.

2.Lightly butter the dough.

3.Sprinkle on desired amount of sugar and press into the dough.

4.Fold both sides of the pastry into the centre twice and then fold in half, creating a log.

5.Cut the log into 1-1.5 centimetre pieces and place flat creating a heart, onto the baking tray.

6.Place cookies into an air fryer and fry for 5-7 minutes or until golden brown

7.Once cool, top with a sprinkle of powder sugar and a drizzle of chocolate.( optional).

8.Enjoy!
