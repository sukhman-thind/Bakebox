RECIPE

CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

Total Time: 2 hours 15 minutes | Yield: 12-14 Cookies

Ingredients




















Method

1.Combine melted butter , white sugar, brown sugar and mix.

2.Make egg replacer ( mix 1 tbsp of egg replacer with 2 tbsp of water). Put aside for a minute.

3.Using a hand blender or whisk. Mis in egg replacer and vanilla extract until mixture is light in colour.

4.Sift in flour, baking soda and salt. Then fold into the mix.

5.Finally add desired amount of chocolate chips and fold them in.

6.Let the dough rest for at least 2 hours, if not over night.

7.Preheat oven to 325F.

8.Scoop out dough balls roughly 2 tbsps and place on to a baking tray.

9.Place tray into the oven and let bake for roughly 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.

10.Let cool and enjoy!
